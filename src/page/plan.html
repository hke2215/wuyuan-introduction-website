📌 Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel or modify my appointment?

You can cancel or modify your reservation free of charge up to 48 hours before departure. Please contact us via the message form or by calling our customer service hotline.

Do you provide English-speaking tour guide services?

We offer bilingual (Chinese and English) tour guides. Please specify your needs in the "Special Requests" section when making a reservation.

When is the best time to visit Wuyuan?

The rapeseed flowers are most spectacular when they bloom in spring (March-April), and the autumn sun-drying in Huangling is unique in autumn (October-November). Each season has its own characteristics.

Are there any discounts for group travel?

Groups of 10 or more can enjoy a 15% discount. Please contact us through the message below or the appointment form for details.

How do I get to Wuyuan?

The most convenient option is to take the high-speed rail to Wuyuan Station. Alternatively, you can transfer to a bus from Jingdezhen or Huangshan Airport and enjoy a package that includes a private car transfer service.
